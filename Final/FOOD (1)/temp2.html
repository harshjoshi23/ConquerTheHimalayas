AALOO TAMATAR JHOL:-

Aaloo Tamatar Jhol is a very easy potato tomato curry made widely in Kumaon. It serves as a simple curry for main course meals or it can be an acompaniment with poori and parathas. This recipe used to my Ammmaâ€™s (grandmother)almost daily meal. She liked this recipe with minimal spices as it was light and tasty. I like it best with pooris accompanied with raita.
Preparation Level: 10 mins to boil potatoes
Cooking Time:15 Mins
Yield: 2 Servings
Difficulty level : easy
Cuisine : North Indian, Kumaoni

INGREDIENTS:-

1. 2 medium sized boiled potatoes
2. 1 medium sized tomato finely chopped
3. 1 medium sized onion finely chopped
4. salt to taste
5. 1/4 tsps turmeric powder
6. 1/2 tsps chilli powder
7. 1 tbsps dried fenugreek leaves
8. 1 tsps carrom seeds/ajwain
9. 1 tsps cumin seeds/jeera
10. 1 tbsps oil
11. water as needed
12. 1 tbsps paste of ginger garlic and green chillies

COOKING STEPS WITH PICTURES:-

1. In a broad pan heat oil and add cumin seeds and carrom seeds. When cumin seeds start to splutter, add one tbsps ginger-garlic-green chillies paste. Add chopped onions and let them cook until translucent in color. Now add finely chopped tomatoes to this.

2. Let the tomatoes cook and then add salt, turmeric powder and red chilli powder.Mix spices well and let them cook for about a minute. Now add chopped boiled potatoes. Usually you cut them in random shape and also crush some boiled potatoes. Mix everything well so that potatoes are well coated with the mixture.

3. Now add around 1/2 cup of water and mix well.Crush the fenugreek leaves in between your palms and spread on top.Mix everything well and let it simmer on low to medium flame for about 5-7 minutes.
