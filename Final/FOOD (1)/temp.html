PUE:-

Pue is a very integral part of Kumaoni cuisine. No pooja or worship can be complete without pue as naivaidya or prasad. Pue are made by soaking semolina in curd. Pue is also made on any festive occasion or birthdays.
Preparation Time: 60 minutes
Cooking Time:20 Mins
Yield : 10 pieces
Difficulty level : medium
Cuisine : Indian, Kumaoni

INGREDIENTS:-

1. Suji or Semolina – 100 grams
2. Curd – 60 grams
3. Milk – 70 grams
4. Sugar – 25 grams
5. Ghee/Clarified butter – 10 grams
6. Oil for frying

COOKING STEPS WITH PICTURES:-

1. In a bowl take suji, sugar and ghee, mix them nicely and keep the mixture aside for at least one hour for soaking. After an hour the suji mixture will become soft. Add milk to the mixture and mix well so that batter is prepared of pouring consistency. It is important to beat the batter with your hands several times for around 10 minutes so that it becomes frothy and air penetrates in it, so that when you fry the pua it can rise.

2. In a pan, heat some oil it is becomes good for frying. You can check this by pouring a small drop of batter in oil. If the drop rises and sizzels, it means oil is ready for frying.
3.Using your fingers drop a small ball of the batter. Let this cook, turn golden from one side and then flip it over. Cook it well from both sides.
