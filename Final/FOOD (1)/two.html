TUKDIYA BHAT:

Tudkiya Bhat is a delicious flavourful rice pulao, cooked using spices like cinnamon, bay leaf, chilli powder, ginger, garlic, cardamom, lentils, potatoes, onions, and yoghurt. Made with a mix of veggies, rice and lentils this is served with a side of mashed dal and a twist of lime. It is perfect comfort food for the chilli mountain weather.

INGREDIENTS:-

1. 1 cup rice
2. 1/2 cup masoor(whole)
3. 2 onion
4. 2 potato
5. 2 tbsp ghee
6. 1/2 cup curd
7. 1 bay leaf
8. 1 cardamom
9. 2 badi elaichi
10. 1 cinnamon stick
11. 1 tomato
12. Ginger and 4 cloves of garlic
13. 2 dagad phool
14. Green chilli
15. Dhaniya leaves
16. Poppy seeds

COOKING STEPS:-

• Grind poppy seeds, tomato, One onion, green chilli, coriander, garlic cloves and ginger, daagad phool, cinnamon stick, one badi elaichi and transfer it into a bowl
• Peel and chop the potatoes and add it into the masala prepared.
• Wash and soak the masoor in water. Set aside for about 30 minutes.
• Wash and rinse the rice. Soak in 2 cups of water.
• Heat the ghee in a cooker add 1 bay leaf,1 cardamom,1 cardamom black, 1 cinnamon stick and sauté and add onions
• After onions have been sauted, add the masala and then the curd after 5 minutes.
• When the oil starts separating out, add the rice nd the daal with 2.5 cups pf water.
• Pressure cook it until you get 3 whistles on high flame.
• Serve hot with a dash of lime or daal of your choice.
