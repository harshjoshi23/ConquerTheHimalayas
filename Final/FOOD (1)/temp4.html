Kumaoni Kheera Raita:-

Kheera raita or cucumber yoghurt is a popular recipe made as a side-dish. However, a traditional kumaoni raita is more spicy and sharp. Though the main ingredients are same cucumber and yoghurt, but there are few extra spices that give the kumaoni raita a sharp edge. “Raita” word is actually made from rai or mustard. Mustard seeds paste is an essential ingredient in the kumaoni raita. Mustard paste with fresh green chillies gives a sharp spicy flavour to the recipe. When you travel through the kumaon hills, you can find small restaurants/dhabas selling thi raita with aaloo ke gutke or aaloo-pyaz pakoras.
Preparation Time: 15mins
Serves:4 Persons
Difficulty level : easy
Cuisine : Indian, kumaoni

INGREDIENTS:-

1. Cucumber – 2 medium sized
2. Curd or plain yoghurt – 250 grams
3. Mustard seeds – 1 tbsps
4. 1 tsps chopped green chillies
5. 1/4 tsps turmeric powder
6. salt to taste
7. 1/4 tsps red chilli powder
8. Finely chopped coriander

COOKING STEPS WITH PICTURES:-

1. Wash the cucumber and grate it. In case cucumber is very ripe, peel of the outer skin. Traditionally the the cucumber grown in kumaon region is bigger in size than a normal cucumber and has lots of seeds and when using it for raita its skin and inner seeds are removed. In a blender make paste of mustard seed using 1 tsps of water.

2. In a bowl take the curd and add salt, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, mustard paste and chopped green chillies to it. Whisk it well so that all the spices are well blended with the curd.

3. Now add grated cucumber to this curd spice mixture and mix well. Finally garnish with some chopped coriander and deseeded green chillis.
