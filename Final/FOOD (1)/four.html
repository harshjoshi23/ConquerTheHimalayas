CHANA MADRA:

Channa Madra is a Himachali dish with a rich blend of chickpea and the goodness of yogurt. It is also one such dish that is prepared by slowly cooking chickpeas in yoghurt gravy added with rice mixture. This dish is easy-to-make and plays a quintessential part of wedding menu in Himachal Pradesh.

INGREDIENTS:-

1. 1 cup Kabuli Chana (soaked overnight and boiled)
2. 1 cup curd
3. 1/2 cup rice
4. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
5. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
6. 1 cup water
7. 3-4 black peppercons
8. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
9. 2 tbsp desi ghee
10. 2-3 cloves
11. 1 tsp heeng
12. 1-2 small cinnamon stick
13. 1 black cardamom and 2 green cardamom
14. 2 tbsp mustard oil

COOKING STEPS:-

• Soak rice with cardamom in water for about an hour and then drain the water and prepare a paste of this rice.
• Grind together black cardamoms, black peppercorns and cloves. It should be granulated.
• Heat oil in a pan on medium flame. Add the granulated mixture along with cinnamon sticks, heeng and sauté.
• Add coriander powder and turmeric powder and sauté for 20 seconds.
• Add the chickpeas and the yogurt and stir constantly to avoid lumps. Let it cook for another 12-13 minutes.
• Once the mixture is properly cooked, add the prepared rice mixture in it and stir and let it cook for another 15-20 minutes. Add the ghee as well.
• Add salt per taste. Turn the heat on low flame and let the dish cook for 35-40 minutes and serve hot!!!
