Kedatkanth Trek:

Decorated with a enchanting magnificence site of fresh powder-soft sparkling snow falling and fringed with pine trees, Kedarkantha trek is perfect for those who are looking for something adventurous. It is an all-weather trek but well known and popular for winter trekking. This trek has mythological importance and some believe that it is related to the ancient script -” The Mahabharata''. The total distance covered during the Kedarkantha trek is about 23 km, with the terrain staying a gentle incline and decline throughout - making it ideal for a first-timer as well.The Kedarkantha Trek is an ideal destination for the nature lovers to bask in the beauty of undisturbed nature and enjoy delightful, camping under the starry sky in tents and blissful moments with exhilarating experiences. The people of the region are said to be friendly and follow a unique culture and practice. Uttarakhand, permeating nature’s glory offers a dramatic landscape dotted with lovely lakes, tranquil rivers, mountains and rising Himalayan peaks.

Kedarkanth trek itinerary:



Day 1: Delhi-Dehradun(5hours)| Dehradun to Sankri (10-11 hrs)
Day 2: Sankri to Juda ka talab (4 km) 5 hrs
Day 3: Juda ka talab to Kedarkantha base (4 km)
Day 4: Kedarkantha base to Kedarkantha peak; descend to Har Gaon (6 km)
Day 5: Har Gaon to Sankri (6 km)
Day 6: Drive back to Dehradun and Delhi
