CHHA GOSHT:-

This is a very delicious mutton curry from the hill state of Himachal Pradesh. Mild in flavour, it is cooked in a yogurt and roasted gram flour based gravy with a wide array of aromatic spices which gives it a slight mild tangy taste. The marinated mutton pieces slowly cooked in gram flour and yoghurt gravy will be a treat to your taste buds.

INGREDIENTS:-

1. 1KG Mutton with bones
2. 4 Bay leaves
3. 7-8-cloves
4. 4-5 medium sized onions
5. 4 kashmiri red chillies and green chillies
6. 2-3 small cinnamon sticks
7. 7-8 green cardmomn
8. 3-4 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
9. ½ tsp heeng
10. 4 cups yogurt
11. 5 tbsp mustard oil
12. 4 tbsp gram flour
13. Salt to taste
14. 2tsp cumin seeds and powder
15. 2 tsp turmeric powder
16. Coriander powder
17. 2 inches ginger

COOKING STEPS:-

• in a bowl take the mutton pieces, add ginger and garlic paste with crushed cloves, cinnamon, bay leaves, cumin powder and cardamom. To this mix add a small portion of yogurt and rub onto to meat to marinate it well. Refrigerate for at least 5 hours.
• Heat 2 tbsp. oil and add cumin seeds. Then fry the ginger and garlic for a minute. Then add the onions and saute till light brown. After this, add coriander powder, heeng , red chilli powder, green chillies and chopped ginger. Saute the spices for 4-5 minutes.
• Dry roast the gram flour and rest of the turmeric powder till you get a nice aroma.
• Add the yogurt to this mixture of gram flour and add some water and bring it to a boil. Transfer this into the onion vessel.
• Now, add the marinated mutton to it cook on a slow flame for about an hour until the mutton softens a bit and is easy to chew.
