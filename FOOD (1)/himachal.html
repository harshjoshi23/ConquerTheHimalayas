CHHA GOSHT

This is a very delicious mutton curry from the hill state of Himachal Pradesh. Mild in flavour, it is cooked in a yogurt and roasted gram flour based gravy with a wide array of aromatic spices which gives it a slight mild tangy taste. The marinated mutton pieces slowly cooked in gram flour and yoghurt gravy will be a treat to your taste buds.

TUKDIYA BHAT:

Tudkiya Bhat is a delicious flavourful rice pulao, cooked using spices like cinnamon, bay leaf, chilli powder, ginger, garlic, cardamom, lentils, potatoes, onions, and yoghurt. Made with a mix of veggies, rice and lentils this is served with a side of mashed dal and a twist of lime. It is perfect comfort food for the chilli mountain weather.

ATKORI

Aktori is an ancient recipe from the dry valleys of Lahaul-Spiti in Himachal Pradesh. This delectable sweet is very easy and quick to make and is typically prepared during festivities and celebratory event. The buckwheat leaves are mixed with the flour of wheat and then prepared in pancakes. Top the dish with honey and ghee or simply serve the dish with hot with chutney.It can be served for breakfast or as a tidbit.

CHANA MADRA

Channa Madra is a Himachali dish with a rich blend of chickpea and the goodness of yogurt. It is also one such dish that is prepared by slowly cooking chickpeas in yoghurt gravy added with rice mixture. This dish is easy-to-make and plays a quintessential part of wedding menu in Himachal Pradesh.
